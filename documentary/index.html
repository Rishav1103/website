"Hello World" Documentary

Sign up to our newsletter to keep updated about the documentary release.

An insight into one of Europe’s most prominent coding bootcamps, ‘Hello World’ explores the journeys taken by a group of ambitious students intent on learning to code in an intensive environment, as well as examining the challenges faced by the startup’s staff and coaches whose mission is to turn the educational industry on its head. ‘Hello World’ will tell its story through a multitude of perspectives. Throughout, the film will focus on the human side of learning to code, the challenges of running a startup and the emotional journeys taken by its ensemble.
